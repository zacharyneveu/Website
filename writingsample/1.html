Will Lossless Music Gain Widespread Popularity?
Draft 3
Introduction
Recently, there has been a growing trend towards lossless music distribution. With the
high proﬁle launch of the Tidal lossless music streaming site and new lossless players like Niel
Young’s Ponos and Sony’s Walkman series being introduced, it may seem as though lossless
music is the next step in audio technology. While lossless music touts perfect sound quality, it is
not very likely to overtake traditional formats as the consumer standard for music.
When websites and devices talk about lossless music, they are referring to a method of
digitally storing audio data. Lossless music is marketed as sounding more ”true to the original” or
having ”perfect quality” but the truth is that to an everyday listener on everyday equipment there
is no audible difference between regular music off of iTunes or Spotify and lossless music. In
order to understand why this is, one must ﬁrst understand some of the basic terms and methods
for storing audio digitally.
